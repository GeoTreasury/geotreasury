Cardano UBI E-commerce Platform

A New Way to Share Wealth and Fight Automation’s Despair

Our Purpose

Automation is displacing workers and fueling inequality. We’re building a Stripe-like e-commerce platform on Cardano that shares 2-10% of profits as Universal Basic Income (UBI) with any Cardano user, restoring hope and equity.

Why It’s Needed

Tech is cutting jobs in retail, logistics, and beyond. Software engineers can solve this by creating systems that share wealth. Our platform gives displaced workers a safety net to retrain, start businesses, or thrive.

How It Works

  • Developers integrate ADA payments via a simple API.
  • 2-10% of profits feed a Plutus smart contract treasury.
  • Cardano’s RNG picks random recipients from Atala PRISM’s identity registry.
  • Developers can restrict UBI to specific countries (e.g., US, India).
  • Recipients get ADA and a message: “someapp.com shared 5 ADA with you.”

Feasibility

Built on Cardano’s low-fee blockchain, with Plutus for secure UBI, Atala PRISM for identity, and Hydra for scaling. We’re prototyping now using Cardano’s testnets and Developer Portal.
